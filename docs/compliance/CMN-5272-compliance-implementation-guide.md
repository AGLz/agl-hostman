## Executive Summary

**Resolution CMN 5.272/2025** represents a paradigm shift in RPPS investment regulation, introducing a **tiered governance system** that links investment flexibility directly to institutional maturity and certification levels. This comprehensive guide provides a structured roadmap for achieving full compliance by February 2028.

### Key Changes from CMN 4.963/2021

| Aspect | Before (CMN 4.963) | After (CMN 5.272) |
|--------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Governance** | Single-tier uniform system | Four-tier progressive system (Nível I-IV) |
| **Investment Limits** | Uniform for all RPPS | Progressive based on Pró-Gestão certification |
| **Asset Classes** | 6 segments | 6 segments (refined and reorganized) |
| **Compliance Deadline** | Immediate | Transition period up to asset maturity |
| **Service Providers** | Approved list | S1/S2 classification required |

### Critical Timeline

| Milestone | Date | Action Required |
|-----------|------|-----------------|
| **Resolution Effective** | February 2, 2026 | ✓ COMPLETED |
| **DPIN 2026 Submission** | April 30, 2026 | Policy alignment required |
| **DAIR Feb/Mar 2026** | April 30 / May 31, 2026 | Transitional reporting |
| **Asset Adjustment Deadline** | February 2, 2028 | Full compliance target |

### 3.2 Portfolio Transition Roadmap

**Phase 1 (Months 0-6): Immediate Compliance**
- Identify all non-compliant assets
- Classify assets by maturity and liquidity
- Create transition schedule
- Establish no-new-investment policy for non-compliant assets

**Phase 2 (Months 6-12): Gradual Adjustment**
- Allow natural maturities to reduce exposure
- Reinvest proceeds in compliant assets
- Maintain target allocations during transition
- Monitor market conditions for optimal entry points

**Phase 3 (Months 12-24): Full Alignment**
- Complete all transitions
- Optimize portfolio for new constraints
- Implement advanced strategies as certification progresses
- Document all compliance activities
