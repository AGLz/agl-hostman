## Main Prohibitions (Art. 28)

1. Funds with derivatives exposure > 1x net worth
2. Assets where the federative entity is issuer/guarantor
3. Non-standard FIDC
4. Day trade operations
5. Index funds in over-the-counter market
6. Qualified investor funds (without criteria)
7. Unforeseen remuneration
8. Non-consigned loans
9. COE (Structured Operations Certificates)

## Research Notes

This resolution was in effect for approximately 4 years and 1 month. It established the investment framework for Brazilian public pension funds (RPPS), introducing a governance-based system where higher governance levels allowed for greater investment limits across various asset classes. The resolution was comprehensive, covering 6 investment segments with specific limits, prohibitions, and governance requirements.

Key innovation: The governance level system (4 levels) that progressively increased investment limits for RPPS demonstrating better management practices certified by the Secretaria de Previdência.
